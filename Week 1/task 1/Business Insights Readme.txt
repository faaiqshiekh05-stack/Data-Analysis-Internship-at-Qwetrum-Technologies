Business Insights

1. Apple Smart Phone, Full Size was the highest-selling product, generating total sales of 86,936, making it the top revenue-contributing product in the dataset.

2. Smartphones dominated the top-selling products list, with Apple, Cisco, Motorola, and Nokia smart phones occupying four of the top five positions.

3. Sales revenue showed a consistent upward trend from 2011 to 2014, indicating strong business growth over the four-year period.

4. November 2014 recorded the highest monthly revenue of 555,312, while December 2014 was the second-highest month with sales of 503,154, suggesting a strong year-end sales surge.

5. Revenue generally peaked during the second half of each year, particularly between August and December, indicating possible seasonal demand and increased customer purchasing activity during those months.
